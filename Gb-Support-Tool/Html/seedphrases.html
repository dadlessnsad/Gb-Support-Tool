Seed Phrases

What are Seed Phrases & how to utilize them?

To simply put it a seed phrase uses a 2048 word dictionary list to form a 12 to 24-word phrase. Each word is assigned to a number the whole phrase together with is our seed integer for deterministic wallet, that creates all of the wallet's key pairs. Using seed phrases makes it easier for the human brain to understand.

It's not recommended to make your own seed phrases, as humans, in general, are bad at randomness. Instead, use the wallet's software and store your seed phrases safely.

Ways to Store Seed Phrases

Just like private keys seed phrases need to be stored securely. It can be written down or stamped in metal. It is not recommended to store your seed phrases in files that are online.(connected to the internet.)
