How do I receive funds?

Receiving funds is as easy as getting an e-transfer! The first thing you'll need is a Crypto wallet, it doesn't matter what kind (Desktop, Web, Hardware, & mobile wallets). After setting up your wallet you will receive two keys, public/private keys. Garb the Public key & keep your private key secret. Your public key acts the same as your bank account number, we'll consider this your address. Easily send the address of the Coin you wish to receive EX;BTC, BCH, ETH, will all have a diffrent address beacuse there build on seperate blockchains.

What is a ENS?

ENS stands for Ethereum Name Service, it is very similar to DNS. ENS adds a secure and decentralized way to add human-readable names to Crypto addresses. Ex; Getnlemensbank.eth would be the ENS for 0x86d49e5484A682E6cc96Cb158f425549ea8fC3C5
